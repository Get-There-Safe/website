Emphasis on Safety

Secure Match Verification

Before confirming any walking match, users must complete a unique One-Time Password (OTP) verification process. This additional security layer ensures that each walking buddy is a real, authenticated individual before any connection is established.

Verified Group Matching

Our smart algorithm prioritizes group walks with 3-4 verified users. Group walks significantly reduce individual risk, creating a safer collective walking experience with built-in social accountability.
